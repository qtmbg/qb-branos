Buffer is a free social media scheduling tool. Connect it once and QB OS queues your content automatically across Instagram, LinkedIn, X, and Facebook.

How to get your Buffer token

1. Create a free account at buffer.com
2. Go to buffer.com/developers/apps
3. Create a new application (any name)
4. Copy the Access Token and paste it above
